Actor and philanthropist, Sonu Sood founded the Sood Charity Foundation to build a brighter path for the poor and underprivileged.

We have launched multiple programs and initiatives digitally and on-ground, to provide better quality education, career opportunities, healthcare services, and relief and rehabilitation during times of crisis with the aim of assisting them to attain self-reliance.

Sood Charity Foundation intends to transform the situations of disadvantaged sections of the society by providing them with the necessary resources to free themselves from their economic limitations. Let’s save lives together.

Change a life or change yourself.

- Sonu Sood
